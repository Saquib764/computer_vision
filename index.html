This series is result of my learning while doing a research project at IIT Bombay under Prof. Srikant Sukumar. The project was related to quadrotor stablisation and path tracking. The project demanded minor form computer vision. We decided to use OpenCV for our purpose and chose python as it was simpler.

Series aims to assist student with almost no background in computer vision to start from basics to advance level

During the process I learned a lot about computer vision. Before the project I have vague little idea about how it is done. And had learned a lot starting from scratch. I hope this would help student/people with similar situation.

PS: I will keep updating further research on computer vision here.

Enjoy :)
